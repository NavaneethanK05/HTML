Rohith Sharma

Cricketer (Batsman)

Rohit Sharma is a celebrated Indian cricketer, known as the "Hitman" for his explosive batting, particularly
his exceptional six-hitting ability in limited-overs cricket, holding records like the highest individual
score in One Day Internationals (ODIs) and multiple World Cup centuries.
